Knot

PRODUCT DESIGN
BACKGROUND

During my freshman year at Brown University, I enrolled in CSCI 0130 User Interfaces and User Experiences. As part of the course curriculum, my team of five, comprising of Ivy Zhang, July Qi, Fiona Liu, Natalie Zhu, and myself, undertook a group project. This case study represents the synthesis of our collective learnings and experiences from this project.

Role
User Research, Sketching, Wireframing, User Testing
Duration
12 weeks
Tools
Figma, Procreate

18 million college students enter college with a roommate. Young adults living with roommates and suitemates struggle with effective communication and management of shared space, often resulting in misunderstandings and negative impacts on a student’s life from their academic performance to their mental health. Our app addresses the issue by providing a communication platform that streamlines requests and task management between roommates.
